About

I made my first website in 1996, which probably ages me, but I'd rather be honest about it.

Most of my career has been inside the Australian Public Service, and most of my evenings and weekends spent making things on my own — music, novels, websites, the occasional small product. The variety isn't a hobby; it's the point.

What I bring to clients is range and old-fashioned reliability. I'll write the words, take the photos, design the site, build the site, and look after it once it's live. No agencies, no account managers, no rooms full of strangers. Just me, doing the work directly.
